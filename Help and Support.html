üí° Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an Area of Interest? ‚ñº
To create an AOI: 1) Go to the Interactive Map page, 2) Click "Draw AOI" button, 3) Click on the map to define your area's boundaries, 4) Fill in the AOI details form, and 5) Click "Save AOI" to store it.
What satellite data sources does ChangeSentinel use? ‚ñº
ChangeSentinel integrates data from multiple satellite sources including Sentinel-2, Landsat 8/9, and commercial high-resolution imagery. We automatically select the best available imagery based on your AOI location and monitoring requirements.
How often is satellite data updated? ‚ñº
Satellite imagery is updated every 3-7 days depending on your location and weather conditions. We automatically process new imagery as it becomes available and notify you of any detected changes.
Can I export my data? ‚ñº
Yes! You can export your data in multiple formats including CSV, GeoJSON, PDF reports, and image archives. Go to the Export Data page to download your monitoring data.
What types of changes can be detected? ‚ñº
Our AI can detect deforestation, urban expansion, agricultural changes, water level variations, infrastructure development, land degradation, vegetation health changes, and more. The detection algorithms are continuously updated and improved.
How do I upgrade my subscription? ‚ñº
Visit the Subscription page from your dashboard. You can upgrade to Pro or Enterprise plans at any time. Your new features will be available immediately, and billing is prorated.
Is my data secure? ‚ñº
Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. All AOIs and monitoring data are private to your account. We're also GDPR compliant and undergo regular security audits.
Can I share my AOIs with team members? ‚ñº
Team collaboration is available on Enterprise plans. You can invite team members, set permissions, and share AOIs and reports. Contact our sales team to learn more about Enterprise features.
